Privacy & Data

Your Data, Your Device

No tracking. No cookies. No servers.

This game collects no personal data of any kind. There are no analytics, no tracking scripts, no cookies, and no external services that receive information about you or your device.

The only data stored by this application is your local game progress — including your current chapter, unlocked chapters, and in-game settings. This data is saved exclusively via your browser's localStorage API, meaning it lives entirely on your own device and is never transmitted anywhere.

You may clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site, or through your browser settings. Doing so will reset your game progress.

No account is required. No login information is stored. No email address or personal identifier is collected or retained anywhere.

This policy is provided in good faith as a statement of intent. This game is a private, non-commercial fan project and does not fall under the regulatory scope of GDPR, CCPA, or similar legislation — however, the creator is committed to respecting your privacy regardless.
